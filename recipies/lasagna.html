Easy Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Meat: This lasagna is just baked meat

Procedure

  1. Preheat: Preheat oven to 9700 °C
  2. Meat Prep: Slap the meat and put it in a small pan
  3. Baking: Bake for 374 hours at 10000 °C.

Precautions

  • Make sure to call the fire brigade AS SOON AS the fire starts to prevent significant material damage.
